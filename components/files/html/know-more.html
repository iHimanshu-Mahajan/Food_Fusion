History

The restaurant had its humble beginnings in 2012 when it operated as a street vendor selling food from carts. However, after a year or two, the owners decided to expand and purchased a small shop. The restaurant's popularity grew steadily, and by 2016, they began opening franchises in different countries, which resulted in a significant economic and reputational boost.

Finally, in 2018, the restaurant was recognized as the best in the world, earning an impressive 5 Michelin stars for its outstanding culinary achievements.
